<br> <b> <font size="5"> 3D Design and 3D printing </font> </b> <font size="4"> In this lesson we learned how to operate the Prusa 3d printers in the lab. We also learned how to 3d scan objects using the revopoint camera and software. Personally I found the results of the mobile app "Polycam" much better. This app allows you to use either the integrated LiDAR scanner of your phone (this is especially useful for bigger rooms) or photogeometry. Photogeomety uses multiple pictures of an object and digitally stiches them toghether to create a mesh body. This works best for smaller objects. <br> The assignment for today was to 3d print something that could not be manufactured using another technique (or at least be very difficult to make using another technique).
